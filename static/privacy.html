Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

The Caizin Policy Assistant is an internal company chatbot designed to provide information related to company policies, leave, holidays, and benefits.

Information Processed

  • User-submitted policy questions
  • Conversation context necessary to generate responses
  • Technical logs for monitoring and improvement

Data Usage

The bot processes queries solely for the purpose of generating policy-related responses. It does not independently store personal data unless required for logging, debugging, or compliance monitoring.

Third-Party Processing

The system may utilize secure AI infrastructure or language model providers to generate responses. No sensitive employee information is intentionally shared beyond what is necessary to fulfill a query.

Data Security

Access to this bot is restricted to authorized internal users. Logging and monitoring mechanisms are implemented to ensure secure and responsible usage.

Contact

For privacy-related concerns, please contact the Caizin IT or HR department.
